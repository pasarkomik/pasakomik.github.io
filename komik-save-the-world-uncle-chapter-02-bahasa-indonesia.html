32. does the horse understand the signals which the writer's uncle gives?jawabkan dengan bahasa Indonesia​


Jawaban:

TEXT NYA

My uncle lives in a village. He has a farm in the village. There is a horse at the farm. Its name

is Hero.

It is a male horse. It has tough body. Its legs are also strong. It has a hairy tail. Its body is covered with bright and short hair. Its colour is brown. It is so pretty. It is very tame, unless someone provokes it. It is herbivore. It only eats grass. It eats a lot; that's why its body is fat and healthy Hero is very clever. It sleeps standing and can run fast. It is able to do certain types of thinking. It can figure out how to open the bolt on the stable door or on the gate. It is always eager to please my uncle as the owner. It can be trained well. It also obeys my uncle's commands. When my uncle whistles,

it comes to my uncle. It also understands some signals which my uncle gives.

SOAL NYA :

1. does the horse understand the signals which the writer's uncle gives?

Answer :

Yes, the horse understand the signal which the writer's uncle gives. It comes to my uncle. It also understands some signals which my uncle gives.

Terjemahan : Ya, kuda mengerti isyarat yang diberikan paman si penulis. Itu datang ke paman saya. Ia juga memahami beberapa sinyal yang diberikan paman saya.
